Bio

Young singer, YouTube'r, actor, lyricist and director Kirill Filatov was born May 1, 1994 in Moscow. Took own way in the pop ensemble Sunny City by Gavrylenko Elena. In late 2010, began his artistic career in the Internet.
May 1, 2011 had a successful debut in iTunes with a single Detskaya. In 2013 released his first solo album and music video. Has won many music competitions, including with Sunny City.
